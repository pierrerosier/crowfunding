About this project

The Mastercraft Bamboo Monitor Riser is a sturdy and stylish platform that elevates your screento a more comfortable viewing height. Placing your monitor at eye level has the potential to improveyour posture and make you more comfortable while at work, helping you stay focused on the task at hand.


Featuring artisan craftsmanship, the simplicity of design creates extra desk space below your computerto allow notepads, pens, and USB sticks to be stored under the stand.
